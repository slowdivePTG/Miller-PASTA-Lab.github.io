title: New type of supernova published in Nature

Our team, led by Steve Schulze, reported SN 2021yfj—an extraordinary event exposing silicon/sulfur-rich layers, offering a direct glimpse into a massive star’s interior.

**Sources:** *Nature* [paper](https://www.nature.com/articles/s41586-025-09375-3.pdf), Northwestern [press release](https://news.northwestern.edu/stories/2025/08/first-of-its-kind-supernova-reveals-innerworkings-of-a-dying-star), ScienceDaily [coverage](https://www.sciencedaily.com/releases/2025/08/250821004234.htm).
